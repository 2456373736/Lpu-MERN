Naming Convention:

camelCase:
Words are joined by making the first letter capital (except the first word) eg: hello ananya -> "helloAnanya"
kebab-case/ hypen-case:
Words are joined by '-' (except the first word) eg: hello ananya -> "hello-Ananya"
snake_case:
Words are joined by '_' (except the first word) eg: hello ananya -> "hello_Ananya"
